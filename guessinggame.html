Welcome to the random number guessing game.

About the game:

  • Here, the computer generates a random number between 10 and 100.
  • The player has to guess any number between 10 to 100 and has to enter it.
  • The computer then shows the number is greater or lesser than the computer generated number.
  • The player will be asked to enter a number until the guess is equal to randomly generated number.
  • According to greater or lesser indication, the player can guess the number speedily.
  • At last, Score will be shown to the player.
